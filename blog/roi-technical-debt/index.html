The ROI of Technical Debt Resolution

Quantifying the cost of legacy code and building a business case for systemic infrastructure modernization.

DET

Dr. Elias Thorne

Strategy

Technical debt is measurable

Debt shows up as slower delivery, higher incident costs, and brittle onboarding. When you quantify those signals, modernization becomes a clear investment.

The business case

  • Tie engineering effort to revenue impact
  • Track opportunity cost from slow releases
  • Use a phased roadmap with visible wins

Start small

Pick one domain, establish a baseline, and show improvement within a quarter.
