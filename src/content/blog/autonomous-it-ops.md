## Automation as a reliability layer

AIOps reduces alert fatigue and shortens recovery time by correlating signals across the stack. The result is fewer outages and faster response.

## Where to apply it first

- Incident triage and routing
- Predictive capacity planning
- Automated remediation playbooks

## Adoption tips

Focus on observability quality before adding automation. Good data makes the tools effective.
