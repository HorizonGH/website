## Why the perimeter fails

Hybrid infrastructure and vendor sprawl make static boundaries ineffective. Zero trust treats every request as untrusted by default.

## Core capabilities

- Identity verification at every hop
- Device posture checks and risk scoring
- Continuous authorization and audit trails

## Migration approach

Start with your most critical apps, then expand with policy templates and automated guardrails.
