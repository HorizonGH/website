## A broader use case

Blockchain is useful when multiple parties need a shared source of truth without central control. Supply chains and identity systems are strong early candidates.

## What to evaluate

- Governance and participant incentives
- Throughput and latency limits
- Integration with existing systems

## Recommended path

Prototype with a single workflow and expand after proving auditability and operational value.
