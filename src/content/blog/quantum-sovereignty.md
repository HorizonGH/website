## The new cloud frontier

Quantum-ready architecture is no longer a lab concept. Enterprise platforms are starting to bake in cryptographic agility, distributed key management, and isolation strategies that can survive future shifts.

## What changes first

- Cryptography needs to be swappable without redesigning the platform
- Data residency requires more precise control and reporting
- Multi-cloud governance becomes a core security concern

## Practical first steps

Start by mapping sensitive workflows, then layer in policy-driven encryption and auditing. The goal is resilience, not perfection on day one.
