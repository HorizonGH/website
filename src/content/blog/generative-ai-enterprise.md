## A grounded adoption plan

Generative AI should start with narrow, high-signal workflows. The fastest wins often live in internal knowledge search, customer support triage, and developer tooling.

## Guardrails that matter

- Keep prompts and outputs inside a controlled data boundary
- Use model routing to reduce risk and cost
- Track usage and drift with policy-based monitoring

## Teams that succeed

Blend product owners, data engineers, and security early. Alignment beats experimentation without a path to production.
